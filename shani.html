About :-
Shaniwar Wada is a historical fortification and palace located in Pune, Maharashtra, India. It was the seat of the Peshwa rulers of the Maratha Empire and played a significant role in Maratha history.
Brief History: -
Shaniwar Wada was commissioned by Baji Rao I, the Peshwa (Prime Minister) of the Maratha Empire, in 1732. The construction aimed to make it the official residence of the Peshwas.
