About :-
The Gateway of India is an iconic monument and a prominent landmark situated in Mumbai, Maharashtra, India. It stands as a symbolic structure representing the rich history and heritage of the city
Brief History: -
The Gateway of India is a historic monument located in Mumbai, Maharashtra, India. Its construction was commissioned to commemorate the visit of King George V and Queen Mary to Mumbai (then Bombay) in 1911, as part of their visit to India to attend the Delhi Durbar. The monument was designed by the architect George Wittet and its construction was completed in 1924.
