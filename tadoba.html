Tadoba Andhari Tiger Reserve, often referred to simply as Tadoba, is a prominent tiger reserve and wildlife sanctuary located in the Chandrapur district of Maharashtra, India. It is one of the largest and oldest national parks in the state, and it is named after the Tadoba Lake.
