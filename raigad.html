About :-
Raigad Fort, located in the Raigad district of Maharashtra, India, is a popular tourist attraction due to its historical significance, stunning architecture, and breathtaking views of the surrounding landscape.
To reach the fort's summit, visitors can take a ropeway, which offers a scenic and convenient way to access the top. The ropeway ride provides breathtaking views of the fort and the surrounding landscape.
Brief History: -
Raigad Fort, also known as Rairi Fort, is a historically significant fortification located in the Raigad district of Maharashtra, India. It holds great historical and cultural importance as it was the capital of the Maratha Empire under the leadership of Chhatrapati Shivaji Maharaj, one of the most celebrated warriors and kings in Indian history.
