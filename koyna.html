About :-
Koyna Wildlife Sanctuary is a protected area and wildlife sanctuary located in the state of Maharashtra, India. It was established in 1985 and covers a significant portion of the Western Ghats, a UNESCO World Heritage Site. The sanctuary is named after the Koyna River, which flows through the region.
